06 — Usage Rules

Do's & Don'ts

Consistent application of the logo and brand elements builds recognition over time. These rules protect the integrity of the ZERO° identity across every touchpoint.

✓
Always Do
  • Use only approved typefaces: Montserrat, Open Sans, and Azeret Mono
  • Keep the ° symbol attached directly to ZERO — no space between them
  • Place the logo only on backgrounds that provide sufficient contrast
  • Maintain minimum clear space equal to the "Z" cap-height on all sides
  • Use ZERO Blue (#0085CA) as the consistent primary brand accent
  • Apply glassmorphism with purpose — one frosted layer per depth plane
✕
Never Do
  • Stretch, compress, rotate, or distort the logo in any dimension
  • Separate "ZERO°" from "Logistics" — they are a single lockup
  • Apply unauthorized colors, tints, or gradients to the logotype
  • Place the logo on low-contrast, busy, or photographic backgrounds
  • Add drop shadows, outlines, bevels, or decorative effects to the type
  • Use the ° symbol as standalone decoration unrelated to the brand name
